**Always be Learning**

I'm always open to learning a new process, tool, or paradigm. I love learning and want to forster that with my teammates. I also know that learning and applying new ideas will cause some me to be wrong sometimes. I am perfectly fine with being wrong so please don't be afraid to tell me. **Systems over goals**

I spent a lot of time trying to acheive goals in my personal life and in that time, I've realized that acheiving goals just helps in the short term and what actually acheives success is a system. A well defined system that can also adapt when things aren't working will help get us to where we need to be. I think working on the system is sometimes just as important as working on towards the goal.

If working in a good system, each task that the system has setup should allow the goal to be hit fairly easily.
